Biography

Zhaokai Li was born in Lishui, China. He received the B.S. degree and Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China, in 2015 and 2020, respectively.
He is currently an assistant professor in Politecnico di Milano, Bovisa Campus. His major research interests include the design and control of electrical machine.

Interests

  • Electromagnetic loss analysis
